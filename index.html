Here to discuss my favorite author, Sarah J. Maas.

Throne of Glass.

Sarah's debut novel sets the stage for her 8 book New York Times Best-Selling Series, Throne of Glass. The installment features 8 books following the main character, Aelin, through her journey from assassian to slave to King's Champion.

A Court of Thorns and Roses.

Sarah's second series is equally as stunning and adventerous as the first. The installment includes: A Court of Thorns and Roses, A Court of Mist and Fury, A Court of Wings and Ruin, and A Court of Frost and Starlight.

Heir of Fire

In the 3rd book, Aelin begins to train to defeat the King of Ardlan with an unlikely ally and soon to be friend.

Empire of Storms

Aelin's plans finally begin to fall into place for her to defeat the King, but a new and worse threat arises. Calling on old favors, Aelin brings together her former enemies and friends for help.

Kingdom of Ash

The final colclusion to the Throne of Glass series follows Aelin through the final battle to reclaim her lost kingdom. As the rightful Queen of Terresan, Aelin reclaims her throne and establishes a new court to change the world.
